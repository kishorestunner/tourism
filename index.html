About One Life Holidayz

More than Travel — We Create Experiences That Bring People Together. Live it. Travel it. Own it.

Let's Go Together

We Started as a Dream, Now We're Your Travel Family

At One Life Holidayz, we don't just plan holidays — we create unforgettable experiences that bring people together. Whether it's a family getaway, a solo adventure, or a strangers group trip, every journey with us is built on joy, warmth, and connection.

We started in 2022 with an Instagram page sharing budget-friendly travel tips and mini vlogs. After two wonderful years building a community filled with thousands of enthusiastic travellers, we officially launched One Life Holidayz on January 1, 2024.

What makes us different is our heart. Our signature "Strangers to Friends" group trips have connected hundreds of solo travellers who now remain lifelong friends. You arrive alone — you leave with a family.
