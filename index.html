Our Story

About Teddy Holidays

More than Travel — We Create Experiences That Bring People Together

Let's Go Together

We Started as a Dream, Now We're Your Travel Family

At Teddy Holidays, we don't just plan holidays — we create unforgettable experiences that bring people together. Whether it's a family getaway, a solo adventure, or a strangers group trip, every journey with us is built on joy, warmth, and connection.

We started in 2022 with our Instagram page @travelwith_teddy, sharing budget-friendly travel tips and mini vlogs. After two wonderful years of building a travel community filled with thousands of enthusiastic travellers, we officially launched Teddy Holidays on January 1, 2024.

What makes us different is our heart. We're not just a travel company — we're a community. Our signature "Strangers to Friends" group trips have connected hundreds of solo travellers who now remain lifelong friends.
